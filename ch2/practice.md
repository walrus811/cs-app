### 2.9.

|  R  |  G  |  B  |  Color  |
| :-: | :-: | :-: | :-----: |
|  0  |  0  |  0  |  Black  |
|  0  |  0  |  1  |  Blue   |
|  0  |  1  |  0  |  Green  |
|  0  |  1  |  1  |  Cyan   |
|  1  |  0  |  0  |   Red   |
|  1  |  0  |  1  | Magenta |
|  1  |  1  |  0  | Yellow  |
|  1  |  1  |  1  |  White  |

A.
Black(000) <-> White(111)
Blue(001) <-> Yellow(110)
Green(010) <-> Magenta(101)
Cyan(011) <-> Red(100)

B.
Blue | Green = 001 | 010 = 011 = Cyan
Yellow & Cyan = 110 & 011 = 010 = Green
Red & Magenta = 100 ^ 101 = 001 = Blue
